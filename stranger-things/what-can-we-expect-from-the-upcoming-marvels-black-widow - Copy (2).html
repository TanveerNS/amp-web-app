What Can We Expect From the Upcoming Marvel’s Black Widow

  • Admin
  • 03/23/2020

Every Marvel fan is well aware of Black Widow, the famous expert tactician female superhero character. The character has made several appearances in various Marvel adaptations. Every appearance has been positively received by the fans. Perhaps that’s the reason Marvel has decided to explore more on the character by dedicating an entire movie to it. Black widow is the latest upcoming superhero film from Marvel Studios. The distribution of the movie will be undertaken by Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures.

Element of Unpredictability

In contrast to the rest of the Avengers team-mates, the character of Black Widow, whose real name is Natalia ‘Natasha’ Romanova, lacks in-depth content. This poses a problem for a theatrical adaptation as the character does not have decades of major comic books to draw material from. If you take a look at the character’s published history, it is rather brief. Most of her published history is either as an Avengers member or as a supporting partner to characters like Hawkeye or Daredevil.

The lack of plentiful content and published history makes the movie highly unpredictable. This makes the movie a nice breeding ground for fan theories. It will be interesting to see what this movie might draw on for inspiration.

Timeline is a Prequel

From what is confirmed, the 2020 Black Widow movie will be a prequel to the events of Avengers: Infinity War. In fact, the events of the movie will take place between those of Captain America: Civil War and the Infinity War.

In case you need fresh reminders, at the end of Civil War, Black Widow was on the run after she assisted Captain America. This help from Black Widow came when Captain America and Winter Soldier were about to face prosecution at the hands of Iron Man’ pro-oversight forces. After this, Black Widow went into hiding until the events of the Infinity War. She then felt compelled to emerge from the shadows due to the imminent Thanos arrival.

The Taskmaster vs Black Widow

According to Marvel, one of the villains of Black Widow will be the Taskmaster. This guy will also be a major negative character in the upcoming Square Enix’s Avengers video game. When we take a look at the comic world, Taskmaster is skilled as a mercenary who possesses the ability to mimic any opponent’s fighting style. His weapon of choice includes a sword and shield. The man is a professional when it comes to hand-to-hand combat. The fight between Black Widow and the Taskmaster will certainly be a mouth-watering contest.

Yelena as a Successor?

A few predictions are possible on the basis of Florence Pugh’s role. When we look at the comic books, Yelena Belova, who is a Pugh’s character, is the result of the Red Room. The Red Room is a secret Soviet training facility that results in the forging of Black Widows. Yelena’s debut in 1999 made her out as a villain to Natasha.

The Red Room happens to be a significant element of the MCU. References were made about the Red Room in Avengers: Age of Ultron and Agent Carter. So, don’t be surprised for it play a major role in Black Widow too. There is also a very real possibility that Natasha’s character will come to a closure in the MCU after Black Widow. In such a scenario, we can expect Yelena to take on the Black Widow’s mantle as a suitable replacement.
